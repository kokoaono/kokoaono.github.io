Neuroplasticity 🧠

  • How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.
    • Our brain is like a sponge. It can absorb information and learn new things through repeated activities and practices.
    If you put mind and effort into learning, the neuron in the brain is strengthened which also stimulate other neurons which makes the brain more active and efficient.
    Therefore, learning new things can activate neurons in the brain and increase the capacity of skills and knowledge in people.

  • How might engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for your own benefit.
    • I will look at new challenges/learning more positive and enjoy the challenge itself.
    Also expect that certain things might be difficult to learn but believe in myself and stay positive even if I struggle to understand at first.

  • What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity.
    • Engage in cognitive exercise to continuously stimulate the brain. Try something new also identify and clarify my passions and goals in life.

    Growth Mindset 👑

  • what is Growth Mindset and why it is relevant?
    • Growth mindset is beliefs people have about learning and intelligence and understand the brain grows with efforts also malleable.
    When people understand effort makes them smarter and stronger, they will put time and effort into new challenges.
    This positive belief can increase connectivity between neurons in the brain which eventually change the experience.
    For example, if you believe your brain can grow then your behaviour change which leads to increase in motivation and achievement.

  • In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?
    • Now I am more aware of how the brain functions, I am more cautious about what I think about learning.
    I do not expect to know the answer or know something straight away because that is a whole point of learning something new.
    Also treat the brain like any other muscles, “if you don’t use it, you lose it”👍.

  • How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?
    • I will try to believe in myself, be curious, not afraid to make mistakes and learn from mistakes throughout any learning journey.
